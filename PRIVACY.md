# Privacy Policy — Nightride.fm Player

_Last updated: 8 June 2026_

Nightride.fm Player ("the app") is an unofficial, open-source player for the
Nightride FM internet radio service, available for macOS, iOS, and Android. This
policy explains exactly what the app does — and does not do — with your data.

## Short version

The app does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There
are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

## What the app connects to

The app makes network connections only for these purposes:

- **Audio stream** — `https://stream.nightride.fm/<station>.mp3`, while you are
  playing a station.
- **Now-playing metadata** — `https://nightride.fm/meta`, while the app is open,
  to show the current track title and artist.
- **Music-service links** — only when you tap a music link, the app opens a
  search URL for Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube in that app or your browser. On
  iOS and Android, tapping Apple Music first performs a public, no-authentication
  lookup at `https://itunes.apple.com/search` to resolve the exact track; it
  requires no account and sends no personal data.
- **Informational links** — only when you tap them, the app opens the Nightride
  FM website, its Discord invite, the developer's website (plocic.dev), or the
  project's issue tracker in your browser.

That is the complete list of connections the app makes.

## What the app does NOT do

- No ads.
- No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or trackers (no Firebase, Sentry,
  Google Analytics, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Segment, AdMob, AppsFlyer, or similar).
- No accounts, logins, or sign-up.
- No collection of personal data, contacts, location, identifiers, or usage
  profiles.
- No third-party advertising or data-broker SDKs.

The app stores only your local preferences (such as your last selected station
and volume) on your own device. This never leaves your device.

## Data shared with third parties

None. Because the app collects no personal data, it has nothing to share or sell.

When you choose to open a music-service or informational link, your request is
handled by that third party (e.g. Spotify, Apple, Google/YouTube, Discord) under
their own privacy policies. The app adds no identifying information to those
requests.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no personal data from anyone, including children.
